About Us
Why Choose Us
Steaming Mugs is a beautiful cafe and lounge located on Minerva Circle in Basavanagudi, Bangalore. The ambiance of the place is elegant and spacious with comfortable lounges and cozy seating area. The exclusive menu they offer is economical and serves a variety of mouth-watering Indian and Continental fast food items along with a range of delicious and steaming coffees and other beverages. The exclusive Hookah menu offers an array of flavorful and aromatic Sheeshas.

What Makes Our Coffee Special

We take immense pride in crafting a coffee experience that is truly special and unforgettable with Exceptional Bean Selection, Artisanal Roasting Proces, Small-Batch Production, Direct Trade Relationships, Custom Blends and Single Origins
